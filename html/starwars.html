STAR WARS

I am a huge fan of Star Wars here are my favorite movies in order from best to worst with reasons why:

  1. The Empire Strikes Back
    • Darth Vader is super awesome
    • Luke Skywalker is finally starting to figure things out a little bit
  2. A New Hope
    • It's the beginning of everything
    • It's a classic and explains the force the best
  3. Revenge of the Sith
    • It's the birth of Darth Vader
  4. Return of the Jedi
    • Luke Skywalker goes full Jedi
  5. Attack of the Clones
    • You get your first look at the clones
    • The Geonosian arena is really cool
  6. The Force Awakens
    • You get to see Star Wars with more modern special effects
    • Some good character setup with Kylo Ren, Finn and Rey
  7. The Phantom Menace
    • Darth Maul and Qui Gon Jinn are in it
  8. Rise of Skywalker
    • Only beats the Last Jedi because of the fight scene with Ben Solo and the Knights of Ren
  9. The Last Jedi
    • Not a lot of progress in the movie, it would be like if the Battle of Hoth was just one movie.
    • Right from the start it ruined some of the character development from the movie before (especially with finn)
